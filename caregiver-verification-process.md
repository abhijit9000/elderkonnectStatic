Trust is not a feature. It is a foundation.

When a family opens ElderKonnect, they are not browsing a catalogue. They are looking for someone they can let into their home, into their daily life, and into the most vulnerable moments of the people they love. That is an enormous act of faith — and we treat it as one.

This is why every single caregiver and service provider who joins ElderKonnect goes through a thorough, week-long verification process before they ever appear in front of a family. No shortcuts. No exceptions.

---

**It Starts With Skills**

We begin by verifying what every provider says they can do. Academic qualifications, professional certifications, specialised training, and relevant experience are all reviewed and validated by our team. We do not take credentials at face value — we confirm them.

**Then Comes Background Verification**

Our legal team conducts a comprehensive background check on every applicant. This includes reaching out to previous workplaces, verifying employment history, and reviewing professional conduct. We look at where they have been, what they have done, and whether their record reflects the standard of care our families deserve.

**Police Clearance Certificate — Mandatory, Without Exception**

Every caregiver onboarded onto ElderKonnect is required to submit a valid Police Clearance Certificate. This is not optional. It is not a recommendation. It is a hard requirement — and until it is uploaded and verified, no family can select that provider for any service. This single step is one of the most important things we do, because it is the clearest signal of our commitment to keeping every home on our platform safe.

**A Week Well Spent**

The verification process takes up to a week — and we believe that week is worth every hour. It is the time we invest so that families do not have to wonder. So that when a caregiver walks through the door, the family already knows: this person has been checked, cleared, and trusted by ElderKonnect before they were trusted with you.
