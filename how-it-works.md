## For Families

Getting the care your loved one deserves has never been simpler. ElderKonnect walks you through every step so you can focus on what truly matters — being there for the people you love.

**Step 1 — Tell Us What You Need**

Create your profile and share the kind of support you are looking for — whether it is daily home care, medical assistance, therapy, or companionship. We keep it simple from the very first click, because your time and peace of mind matter.

**Step 2 — Find Your Match**

Our AI-powered matching system recommends the most suitable verified caregivers and providers based on your specific needs, location, preferences, and schedule. No guesswork, no overwhelm — just the right person, at the right time.

**Step 3 — Book, Connect, and Relax**

Choose your provider, confirm your booking, and let ElderKonnect handle the rest. Track sessions in real time, communicate directly, and know that help is always just a tap away. From the first hello to ongoing care, we are with you every step of the way.

## For Caregivers Joining Us

ElderKonnect is built for professionals who take their work seriously. If you are ready to bring your skills to families who truly need them, here is how you get started.

**Step 1 — Create Your Profile**

Sign up and build your professional profile the right way. Share your academic background, certifications, skill sets, and upload the necessary documentation. Think of it as your professional identity on a platform that takes credibility seriously. Once submitted, your profile enters our verification queue.

**Step 2 — We Do Our Part**

Our team reviews every profile thoroughly. We go through your submitted documents, run a comprehensive background verification, and ensure that everything checks out before giving you the green light. This process exists to protect you just as much as it protects the families you will serve. If everything is in order, your profile gets approved and you are officially part of the ElderKonnect community.

**Step 3 — Go Live and Start Receiving Queries**

Once approved, log in to your account, set your availability, add your bank details, and define your service preferences. From here, interested families will begin reaching out to you directly. You manage your schedule, you choose what works for you, and the ElderKonnect team remains present end to end — supervising, supporting, and making sure your experience on this platform is as smooth and rewarding as possible.
